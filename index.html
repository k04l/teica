Our Charge

We exist to form people who can form places.

TEICA teaches the precise techniques of ecological cultivation — soil, water, plants, animals, structures — while deliberately developing the judgment, patience, and virtue required for long-term stewardship.

We begin with those who already work the land or wish to. We end with communities and institutions capable of caring for California’s landscapes for generations.
